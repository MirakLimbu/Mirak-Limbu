Introduction To Internet




The internet is the network of network that consists of millions of public, private, government, business and many more networks which uses standard internet protocol suite (TCP/IP) to provide/deliver information for billions number of users. The internet holds a vast range of information resources and services, linked with hypertext document of World Wide Web (WWW).



History:


History Of Internet:

  • It was created as ARPANET by the United State (US) department of defense.
  • In 1962, for the purpose of communicating between the government leaders, a scientist from M.I.T. and ARPA named J.C.R. Licklider proposed a solution to this problem.
  • In October 29, 1969 the first information/message was sent.
  • The first message/information sent was “LO” which was attempt at “LOGIN” by Charley S.Kline to log into the computer from University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA).
  • Later, In year of 1980s this networking infrastructures was made available publicly.



By the use of internet, users can communicate from one place to another and also share data in a second of time. It helps in the field of studying, researching, communication, viewing news, information and data exchange and many more. In this pandemic situation, Internet has played a huge role in education system. By the help of internet, online class started where no one has to go anywhere for attending a class but must be connected with internet. The internet is very easy to use and, Once someone learnt how to use internet then they can use search engines to find almost everything.



Advantages/positive impact of Internet in daily life:


  • It supports communication between two or more users, using emailing and instant messaging services to any part of the world.
  • By the use of internet, users can do online jobs like (Video editing, web designing, book writing and many more).
  • You can view latest news through the medium of google, facebook, youtube and others.
  • In education system, Internet has boost it up too ahead. Students, teachers can view books, articles, journals from libraries across the world by doing research.
  • Things like paying, shopping can be done online.
  • Internet helps to kill boring time using it for entertainment purposes.
  • Location access. Applications like (Google maps, Waze) provides geographical mapping information.


Disadvantages/Negative impact of Internet in daily life:


  • During this pandemic situation, the teaching process in going through online system which lacks face to face communication.
  • Lack of creativity by the excessive use of internet for unusual purposes.
  • Chances of data leakage.
  • When students/teenagers use internet too much, the abandonment of family will occur.
  • For young people at this ages, their personality, as well as moral are in the stage of development and the impropriate information on the internet can cause moral corruption to them.
  • Personally and professionally life can be damaged by getting addicted to social networks.
  • Availability of illegal materials online that isn’t age-suitable.


However having some negative impacts of internet, the negative impact can be reduced by following listed suggestions:

Measures to Controll:



  • Limit the use of internet.
  • Spend more time with friends and families and maintain more real life relationship than internet relationship.
  • Change the pattern of communication. Meet them, talk with them face to face more than by online.
  • If you are teenagers/students follow a routine. Time management is the best way to reduce internet addiction.
  • Prioritise your Needs. IF you are a students, at first you complete all you works or if you are a businessmen you can prioritise your meetings schedule at first.
  • If you are getting addicted to internet or grtting out of control you can take serious action by getting rid of your devices for a fixed time period.
  • There are more things you can do without the use of internet. Try to play physical game more than mobile/computer games..
